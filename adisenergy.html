How it works

The key ingredient is the ceramic composite material. Its nano-pores discharge moisture by gravitational pressure and capillary force. Natural evaporation and condensation of moisture from the expanded surfaces of the material draws from and releases heat energy to the surrounding objects - resulting in reduced or increased temperature and humidity.

  • Can reduce the temperature of air from over 40 C to under 30 C without electricity at RH of over 65%
  • Can keep devices running at room temperature using their waste heat.
  • Room temperature tracks material temperature at any relative humidity.
